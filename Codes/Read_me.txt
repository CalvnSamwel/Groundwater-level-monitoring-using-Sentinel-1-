In this file codes and manuals highlighting the steps undertaken in the internship study "Exploring the reliability of Satellite Soil Moisture for Groundwater monitoring" are stored.

1. GGE_Manual_20190304: Provides a manual for the change detection method in google earth engine, JavaScript
2. OWAS1S_CD_S1: Is the JavaScript code used for the retrieving Volumetric Soil Moisture content from Sentinel-1 backscatter time series
3. Low_Pass_Filter_Vitens.ipynb: A Jupyter notebook including all the steps taken to convert Volumetric Soil Moisture content to Volumetric Soil Water Index and finally to estimation of groundwater time series.
